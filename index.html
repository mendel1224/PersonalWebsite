About

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Mendel Shifrin, and I am a dedicated and passionate software engineer with a focus on full-stack development. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine, where I am actively involved in the Google Developer Student Club and have been recognized on the Dean’s Honor List.

Before attending UC Irvine, I completed my studies at Moorpark College with a GPA of 3.66, earning a place on the Dean’s List and participating in the Honors Program and Computer Science Club.

My professional experience spans multiple roles, including a SaaS Entrepreneurship Intern at Corent Technology, where I led the development of EasyKimai, a multi-tenant SaaS application. I also founded and currently operate a successful E-commerce business specializing in athletic footwear and apparel, with over $850,000 in total revenue since inception. Additionally, I interned as a Software Engineer at Odyssey App, designing and implementing a database for a travel recommendation application and working in an Agile Scrum environment.

One of my notable projects is the NASA Psyche Web App Experience, where I served as a Front End Developer. I utilized JavaScript, React.js, and Three.js to create an immersive 3D web application aimed at enhancing public engagement and understanding of the NASA Psyche mission.

My technical skills include proficiency in languages such as Java, C++, Python, and JavaScript, as well as experience with developer tools like Git, AWS, VS Code, and Jira.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my work, projects, and how I can contribute to your team
